The Industry

100,000+ clinics.
One outdated workflow.

You're not the only clinic dealing with this. Across Europe, the manual orthotic workflow is the standard — not because it's good, but because nothing better has been built for clinics at this scale. That's what we're changing.

01

The problem is widespread

Over 100,000 podiatry, physiotherapy, and orthopedic clinics across Europe still rely on manual impression methods developed decades ago. Every clinic feels the same friction — the tools just haven't kept up.

02

Demand for custom care is growing

Aging populations, rising sports participation, and growing clinical awareness of biomechanical conditions are driving more patients toward custom orthotic solutions. The clinics with faster, better workflows will capture that growth.

03

Digital fabrication makes this possible now

3D printing and distributed manufacturing have matured to the point where precision fabrication is fast, affordable, and scalable. The technology is ready. The only missing piece was the platform to connect it to your clinical workflow.

The clinics that adopt first will set the standard of care in their region — and see the workflow, capacity, and outcome benefits while competitors are still doing it by hand.
